### **What is Pageant in PuTTY?**  
ðŸ”¹ **Pageant** (**PuTTY Authentication Agent**) is a tool that **manages SSH private keys** and provides automatic authentication for PuTTY, PSCP, and other SSH-based applications. Instead of selecting the private key manually in PuTTY every time, Pageant loads it **once** and keeps it in memory, allowing PuTTY to use it automatically.
